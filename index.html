Oh, Crystals!


Objective: Match your score to the random computer generated number.


Instructions:


  1. Each crystal will be worth different points.
  2. Clicking a crystal will add an amount to your current score.
  3. You win if you match the computer's random.
  4. The game will restart after you win or lose
  5. The new game will have a brand new number and the crystals will have a different value.
